Welcome to Meridian City!

Here are some key dates to remember for September

Meridian City Food Bank Donations / September 4th

Meridian City Food Bank will be taking walk in donations on September 4th. These donations must be made up of non-parishable food items, any perishable food item will be refused by staff. We had a great turnout last year and hope to see the same or even better results this year!

Community Park Clean up Registration / September 16th

Monday, September 16th will be the last day to sign up for community park clean up. All groups signed up will receive their designated park on September 18th and must contect Robert Evans if there are any issues.

Saturday in the Park / September 28th

Saturday in the Park is here! It is once again time for Saturday in the Park and we look forward to seeing everyone there! There will be live music, food vendors, and a bunch of other activites for all ages.

Doggie Playdate in the Park / September 29th

Sunday is Doggie Playdate in the Park. Bring your furry friend so they can have a playdate with their friends. All dogs must be trustworthy around other dogs and nonreactive. Doggie bags will be provided for clean up.
