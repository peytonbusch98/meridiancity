Contact Information

Meridian City is a place that is always changing and to keep up with that change we have multiple city officials who are there to help you find anything or answer any questions about meridian city.

Mayor Robert Evans

Robert Evans is the current mayor of Meridian City and a great resource to all those who wish to know more about the governmental side of things here in Meridian City. To reach Mayor Evans, his email address can be found at the bottom of this page and responds to almost all emails within forty eight hours.

City Planner Amy Hoover

Amy Hoover is a experienced planner for all things public to the city. Amy has worked for the city for thirteen years now and has planned everything from park clean up to the city founding anniversary every year. To reach Amy Hoover you can contact her at:

amyhoover@california.org

Want to report a problem?

You can contact a city employee to report any sort of problem at the email address listed below. These problems can be anything from power outages, water line ruptures, and pest control as well as anything in between. Just send an email and a city employee will be out to help fix any problem you are facing.

meridiancityproblems@california.org

Mayor Robert Evans, meridiancity@california.org
